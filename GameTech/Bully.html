About The Game

Bully: Scholarship Edition takes you back to school as Jimmy Hopkins, a troublesome teenager who's been expelled from every school he's ever attended. Now stuck at Bullworth Academy, Jimmy must navigate the social hierarchy of the school while dealing with bullies, teachers, and the local townies.

The Scholarship Edition features improved graphics, additional missions, new classes, and multiplayer modes. Experience the open-world gameplay that Rockstar is famous for, but set in the unique environment of a boarding school. Attend classes, play pranks, defend yourself against bullies, and explore the town of Bullworth in this critically acclaimed title.
